About the company

One day, my uncle needed assistance with something so I reluctantly agreed to help him out. After successfully completing his task, the phone calls I once recieved from him every blue moon, suddenly started to pour in once, twice and three times a day! All of the calls being random requests he needed help accomplishing. After about a week and a half of these continuos daily calls, I finally had to spill it... "Uncle! What is up with you asking me to do all these random requests for you everyday! Am I your new personal sidekick??" We both started laughing, but soon realized the same thing... "Random Requests" © was a catchy title ..and a catchy business!
