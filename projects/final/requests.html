Requests

You can request to have almost anything done for you!

  • Interior painting/Exterior painting
  • Personal Assistant work
  • Photography
  • Party planning
  • Buying/Selling items
  • Job Search
  • Match-Make dating
  • Schedule planning
  • Anything you can think up!

You can place a request by emailing: mcgeecor@msu.edu. Please describe your request, put the date you need it done and enter any other information you may find necessary. We will email you back with further information and a pricing scale for your request.
